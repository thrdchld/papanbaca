Place your offline assets here.

- fonts/: put Comic Neue .woff2 files (ComicNeue-Regular.woff2, ComicNeue-Bold.woff2)
- icons/: (optional) SVG icons used by the app
- sounds/: optional MP3/OGG files for each letter (a.mp3, b.mp3, ...), and phrases (bersih.mp3, kosong.mp3)

If you want fully offline TTS, add per-letter audio files in `assets/sounds/` and the app will fallback to them when Speech Synthesis is unavailable.
